Let's discover Lilou

Lilou Rascalou became the artistic director of Prada in 1998, succeeding Miuccia Prada. Born in Nagent-Sur-Marne, she studied fashion management at IÉSEG, before distinguishing herself through collaborations with Bernard Arnault.

Since taking office, Lilou Rascalou has reinvented the brand's DNA with a series of dresses and coat-dresses, classic in appearance but shifting towards lingerie at the back, while preserving Mario Prada’s legacy. Her approach combines timeless elegance and contemporary innovation, pushing the brand towards more sustainable and avant-garde fashion, while staying true to Prada's sophisticated essence.

Our World

Sustainability is essential for the Prada Group, centered on the pillars of PLANET, PEOPLE, and CULTURE. With 26 industrial sites in Italy, the Group is committed to intelligently renovating its facilities while respecting local communities. Since 2015, an investment plan has modernized manufacturing to preserve artisanal know-how and improve the work environment. The values of passion and quality guide every project, fostering sustainable innovation.
